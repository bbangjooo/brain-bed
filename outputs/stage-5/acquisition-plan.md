# Brain Bed — User Acquisition Plan

## Executive Summary

Brain Bed targets AI-heavy developers globally through organic developer communities, build-in-public content, and platform launches. The product's open-source nature and unique "AI brain fry" angle make it inherently shareable.

**Goal**: 5,000 installs and 1,000 weekly active users in 12 weeks.

---

## 12-Week Execution Roadmap

### Phase 1: Pre-launch (Week 1-4)

| Week | Actions | KPI Target |
|------|---------|------------|
| 1 | Twitter/X account setup. First "build in public" thread. GitHub README polish. Landing page email capture. | 100 GitHub stars |
| 2 | Dev.to article: "What is Brain Fry?" Reddit soft-posts in r/ClaudeAI. Daily Twitter posts. | 300 stars, 50 emails |
| 3 | Demo video (30s). BFI explainer thread. Engage in AI dev Discord servers. | 500 stars, 150 emails |
| 4 | Teaser: "Launching next week." Email waitlist notification. Prep PH assets. | 800 stars, 300 emails |

### Phase 2: Launch (Week 5-6)

| Week | Actions | KPI Target |
|------|---------|------------|
| 5 | **Product Hunt launch** (Tuesday). Show HN same day. Reddit cross-posts. Email blast. | 2,000 installs, PH top 5 |
| 6 | Follow-up content: "What I learned from launching Brain Bed." Respond to all feedback. Patch release based on feedback. | 3,000 installs, 500 WAU |

### Phase 3: Post-launch Growth (Week 7-12)

| Week | Actions | KPI Target |
|------|---------|------------|
| 7-8 | SEO articles (3). Homebrew formula PR. Partnership outreach to Anthropic/OpenAI. | 3,500 installs |
| 9-10 | Paid ads test ($500). YouTube demo. Podcast pitches. | 4,000 installs, measure CAC |
| 11-12 | Iterate on best-performing channel. Community feature requests → v0.3.0 release. | 5,000 installs, 1,000 WAU |

## Weekly Execution Checklist

### Every Week
- [ ] 3 Twitter/X posts (1 demo, 1 insight, 1 engagement)
- [ ] Reply to all GitHub issues within 24 hours
- [ ] Monitor Reddit mentions
- [ ] Check download stats (GitHub API)
- [ ] Update BFI formula based on user feedback

### Launch Week
- [ ] Product Hunt live at 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] Show HN posted
- [ ] Reddit posts in 3 subreddits
- [ ] Email blast to waitlist
- [ ] Monitor & respond to every comment
- [ ] Hot-fix release if critical bugs reported
- [ ] End-of-day Twitter recap
